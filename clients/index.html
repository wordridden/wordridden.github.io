I work with a range of clients, including individual authors, publishers, small translation agencies, foundations, and educational institutions. I specialize in the humanities and social sciences, but I also have extensive experience with commercial translations for a variety of companies.

Past and present projects

I’ve collaborated on the translation, copy-editing, and proofreading of the permanent exhibitions at:

  • The Arolsen Archives - International Center on Nazi Persecution (2019)
  • The Wolfenbüttel Prison Memorial (2016)
  • The Ravensbrück Concentration Camp Memorial (2013)
  • The Sandbostel POW Camp Museum and Memorial (2013)
  • The Bullenhuser Damm Memorial (2011)
  • The Bergen-Belsen Concentration Camp Memorial (2007)
  • The Neuengamme Concentration Camp Memorial (2005)

I continue to work with several of these institutions and memorials on the translation of special exhibitions, essays, web content, conference proceedings and other publications.

In addition, I regularly handle small- and large-scale translations (press releases, presentations, magazines) for companies and organizations in diverse industries, and I have translated film subtitles and voiceover texts.
